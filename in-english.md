# MY THEORY OF EVERYTHING IN THE MCU: The Avengers’ Original Sin, the True Logic of the Multiverse, and the Path to Doom’s Battleworld

Many people blame Doctor Strange, the Scarlet Witch, or the collapse of the TVA for the imminent destruction of the Multiverse. But the reality is much more cynical: **Earth-616 (the MCU) dug its own grave all the way back in *Avengers: Endgame*.** 

Through an excess of free will, quantum piracy, and metaphysical chaos, our main heroes became the single greatest cosmic threat to existence. This is the physics and the definitive chronicle of how they broke the board.

---

### I. The Physics of the Multiverse: The Offset of the Cosmic Rings

To understand the disaster, we must first tear down the biggest lie told in *Endgame*: the Avengers never traveled to the past of their own reality. As "He Who Remains" hinted at in *Loki*, the Multiverse functions as **concentric, parallel threads or rings spinning in place**. 

![Multiverse view](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/lncproducciones/my-all-of-everything-marvel-theory/refs/heads/main/002.jpeg)

Each ring is a self-contained macrocosm with its own mass, its own Infinity Stones, and its own closed mystical ecosystem. This includes **their own astral planes, alternate dimensions, and underworlds** (Dormammu's Dark Dimension, the Egyptian Duat from *Moon Knight*, or the Ancestral Plane from *Black Panther* belong exclusively to the lane of their specific ring).

Why did the Avengers believe they were visiting "their past"? Because those rings run in a **temporally offset sequence**. 

Imagine a group of cars on a circular racetrack: they are all in different lanes, but some are a few meters behind. When Stark and his team used the Quantum Realm, they didn't move backward in their own lane; they jumped horizontally into a neighboring lane that simply happened to be passing through a moment equivalent to their "year 2012" or "year 2014". By altering those rings, the Avengers committed the very first act of interdimensional piracy in history.

### II. The 616 Anomaly: A Universe Without a Shepherd

Here is where the real danger of our universe is revealed. In the Marvel macrocosm, each cosmic ring matures and stabilizes when its respective Kang variant builds a TVA to regulate the flow of its time and "prune" anomalies. However, the native Kang of Earth-616 never reached that stage: he was betrayed by his variants and exiled to the Quantum Realm during his primitive phase as **"The Conquistador"**. 

![Courtesy of wallpapercave.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwallpapercave.com%2Fwp%2Fwp11991073.jpg&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=03442399def2ff04020c0d80b62d6d1f7c7ab3e0332fe8d377eb0710a8583174)

By being defeated down there by Ant-Man before he could mature, Earth-616 became a total aberration: **a universe without a native TVA to police it**. Its inhabitants have enjoyed an absolute excess of free will, granting themselves the luxury of altering space-time and metaphysics in ways that would have been instantly erased in any other ring.

#### The Domino Effect in television and film:

![Courtesy of mademoiselleruta.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fstatic-secure.guim.co.uk%2Fsys-images%2FGuardian%2FPix%2Fpictures%2F2014%2F3%2F14%2F1394797385926%2FAgents-of-SHIELD-returns-014.jpg&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=e0274b31f2b656bfa5766e1be00db7856b4df832cb98b60698d5a342c5372a1e)

* ***Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D.***: In any other controlled lane of the multiverse, Nick Fury using Project T.A.H.I.T.I. to revive Phil Coulson would have been cataloged as a **criminal nexus event**. A foreign TVA would have instantly manifested to prune the line and eliminate the resurrected Coulson. But in the unprotected 616, the anomaly thrived, allowing that temporal branch of the same tree to grow freely. That is why, at the end of the series, they could use the Quantum Realm to "return" to the root of their own reality: because they never changed rings, they inhabited a branch left unchecked by the absence of a guardian Kang.

* **The X-Men Universe (Fox)**: This neighboring ring *do* have a master: it belongs to **Rama-Tut**, the Kang variant who took on that Egyptian identity in his own reality in an obsessive bid to "tame" and control the first mutant, En Sabah Nur (Apocalypse). To achieve this, Rama-Tut’s TVA works in the shadows constantly organizing, reorganizing, and shifting the timelines of that universe (which perfectly explains the catastrophic and otherwise inexplicable timeline jumps between *Days of Future Past*, *Apocalypse*, and *Dark Phoenix*). It is a TVA trapped in an eternal loop trying to subdue a mutant god—something that will never happen—giving the X-Men's Earth an erratic and unique temporal behavior.

Meanwhile, the ultimate victor of the wider multiversal war in the outside void was **Immortus**, the supreme leader of the Council of Kangs, who alongside Rama-Tut governs the TVAs of the other rings. That is why, when Sylvie assassinates "He Who Remains" (a pariah Kang who built an isolated bunker around the 615/616 axis using Alioth) and kicks Loki through a portal, Loki doesn't return home: he falls into the TVA of a neighboring ring that answers directly to Immortus, where the Conqueror's statue was always exposed. The walls fell, exposing the free and chaotic 616 universe to the true rulers of the multiverse.

### III. The Metaphysical Collapse: The Spiritual Plunder of Endgame

Operating without TVA supervision, the Avengers' "Time Heist" didn't just steal physical items; it caused an **unprecedented spiritual and dimensional disaster within Earth-616**, breaking the laws of life and death:

* **The Soul Duplication (The Gamora Case):** The original Gamora of 616 died as a sacrifice on Vormir, sending her soul to the pocket dimension of that specific lane's Soul Stone. However, the Avengers dragged the Gamora from the offset 2014 ring into 616. Currently, 616 sustains a horrifying biological paradox: **Gamora's soul is dead in the astral plane of this universe, while her body from another reality walks alive in the physical plane of the exact same universe.**

![Courtesy of screenart.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fstatic1.srcdn.com%2Fwordpress%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2018%2F05%2FGamora-and-Thanos-in-Avengers-4.jpg&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=26f81c9ef6fef9d2f5081fc63910fa0d8c1de092cabda43df0f9cda376dc230b)

* **The Cosmic Gridlock (The Thanos Case):** The original 616 Thanos was decapitated and his soul moved on. But the Thanos from the 2014 ring crossed the quantum bridge into 616, where Iron Man snapped him into dust. By dying physically inside 616, the spiritual energy of this second Thanos became trapped in a foreign astral plane. The 2014 universe was left permanently mutilated (left without its Thanos and without its stones), while the metaphysical planes of 616 are now saturated with anomalies that do not belong to its lane.

### IV. The Multiversal Cancer: Rifts, Webs, and Buffers

Capitalizing on the lack of a stabilizing Kang, the heroes of 616 continued to batter the multiversal membrane bi-directionally, infecting the rest of the board:

![Courtesy of thedirect.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.thedirect.com%2Fmedia%2Fphotos%2Fmcusd.jpg&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=d109b9b9e3a75121236a1677be200fab2da1af0bfcba98359324480540ed86ff)

* **The Great Mystical Earthquake (*No Way Home*):** Strange's failed spell tore open the sky of New York. Because there was no native TVA to patch the tear, it generated a **massive bi-directional leak**. Inwardly, it pulled foreign matter (leaving a piece of Venom’s symbiote at a bar). Outwardly, the mystical current was so violent it expelled 616 natives into other rings: it blasted Adrian Toomes (Vulture) into *Morbius*' universe, and sucked in **Aaron Davis (the Prowler played by Donald Glover)**.

![Courtesy of screenart.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fstatic1.srcdn.com%2Fwordpress%2Fwp-content%2Fuploads%2F2023%2F06%2Fdonald-glover-prowler-and-miles-morales-in-spider-man-across-the-spider-verse.png&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=a5bc669d8dc7d8122849fd551f84ec27f4e96d3f27d954b175c740f42c237cb7)

* **The Collapse of the Web of Life:** Aaron Davis fell through the cracks straight into the **Web of Life and Destiny** (the infrastructure connecting all spider-beings), ending up locked away as a maximum-security anomaly in the animated universe of **Miguel O'Hara**. Neither Madame Web with her clairvoyance nor Miguel with his 2099 tech understand the root cause; they only see the symptoms of the Web fraying, forcing Miguel to build a bootleg TVA (the Spider-Society) to stitch together the threads that the MCU is snapping from a distance. That is why this Prowler is the only purely *live-action* character caged there: he is the visual representation of the dense 616 fabric invading the other animated buffer lanes.

![Courtesy of wallpapers.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwallpapers.com%2Fimages%2Fhd%2Fmarvel-desktop-mcu-what-if-sjcyo4jszvdwxv85.jpg&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=56c1e09f7c02a03a98e2491ffb5b41a202a6b0fe91f77a9a1a1e26ef650d8e7f)

* **The *What If...?* Buffer:** This level of pure chaos explains why the macrocosm activated a desperate emergency defense mechanism. The universe of *What If...?*, with its infinite animated realities, functions as a **buffer ring** designed to absorb, branch out, and process the excess quantum energy generated by 616. This is why **Uatu the Watcher** is constantly terrified. While his brothers observe the Multiverse with cold indifference from their secure lanes, Uatu is the one assigned to the 616 sector. He saw Infinity Ultron smash through the walls, proving the buffer is failing. His oath was broken because the apocalypse is bleeding through.

### V. The Climax: The Birth of 'Battleworld' in *Doomsday* and *Secret Wars*

Our universe has become a radioactive magnet that, through quantum gravitational physics, is drawing all other cosmic rings toward the same central point simultaneously. 

The absolute confirmation of this danger arrives in the post-credits scene of ***Thunderbolts\****. A 14-month time jump shows our new heroes dealing with an imminent "space crisis": quantum physics collapsing and a **massive, multiple Incursion** active in the sky. The arrival of **The Fantastic Four’s** ship is the final nail in the coffin; their retro-futuristic universe has just been destroyed by the contraction of the rings, and they are crossing the rift as cosmic refugees.

![Courtesy of thedirect.com](https://external-content.duckduckgo.com/iu/?u=https%3A%2F%2Fimages.thedirect.com%2Fmedia%2Fphotos%2Ff4ship.png&f=1&nofb=1&ipt=83c2f8bc4a8d7a2f136b532d28bbaa0f4e648ab2a4ecb752037dd419e3e22663)

The damage caused by Earth-616 is so devastating, and there are so many universes colliding in a chain reaction at the same time (Earth-838 scarred by Wanda, the X-Men universe stuck in Rama-Tut’s loop, the Fantastic Four’s world, and the very fabric of the Web of Life), that the entire Multiverse will be reduced to floating debris in the Void.

This total collapse is exactly where **Doctor Doom (Emperador Doom)** will seize his opportunity in *Avengers: Secret Wars*. With no single universe left standing with a stable timeline, Doom will use his power to collect the surviving fragments, patches, and remnants of each of these rings destroyed by the massive collision to stitch them together into a single map of cosmic survival: **Battleworld**. 

The Avengers thought they saved the day in *Endgame*, but their interdimensional arrogance completely shattered the board, leaving behind the perfect pieces for Doom to build his own empire on the ashes of reality.
